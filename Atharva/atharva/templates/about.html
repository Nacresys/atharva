Who We Are

Founded in the Year 2004, under the leadership of travel industry veterans who operated highly successful travel business and understood the necessity and importance of technology in order to standout in an overcrowded market. Started in Mumbai- India we opened our second location at UAE -Dubai in 2018. Over these years, our main focus has been on helping clients to achieve their goals through their websites/portals.


Atharva Infotech has broad experience of working with clients across travel & tourism industries globally. We aim to always deliver the highest level of service and technology solutions to help our clients/ customers succeed in their business.

What We Do

Atharva Infotech provides business solutions to the travel industry based on .NET technology. Our solutions are both flexible and modular in design and can be easily integrated with your existing front or back office systems. All of our products are developed with a modern 'look and feel' and are fully Internet enabled, offering all users web browser access.


A continuous process of design and development ensures that our products are aimed at meeting the needs of today’s tour operator, whether they offer prepackaged tours, specialist tours or dynamically packaged travel options.

Our Team

A team of young, dynamic and talented IT and Travel professionals who will understand your work ethic and hold your hand throughout your journey of growth. Continuous Online support till you are comfortable with the product is a norm which we follow very smoothly.

Our Vision


Understand the ever-changing travel industry needs and provide best-of-breed travel business solutions.
